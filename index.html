My Mission

Design with purpose. Learn with reflection. Grow with intention.

"When you see a good move, look for a better one."

Emanuel Lasker

Former World Chess Champion

This quote reflects how I approach design — and life. I don't settle for "good enough." I believe in pausing, evaluating, and crafting the best possible outcome. This mindset is at the heart of my mission:

"I don't just create websites that look visually appealing — I design to empower Indonesia's UMKM and bring them closer to the digital exposure."

Indonesia is home to more than 65 million UMKM, contributing over 60% of our GDP — yet many still struggle to compete in the digital space. Lack of access, limited tech understanding, and low online visibility hold them back — and that's where I come in.

Why Web Designer?

I chose to become a web designer because it's where creativity meets impact. By combining aesthetics, usability, and empathy, I help businesses tell their story online — clearly and confidently. With the right design, even the smallest brand can look credible, trustworthy, and professional.

I want to become the bridge between UMKM and the digital world — to give them a platform, a voice, and a better chance to thrive. I believe that every action, even the slightest ones, will contribute to Indonesia's overall development, especially in achieving SDGs #9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure.
